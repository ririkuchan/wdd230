The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

1. Site Name

Proposed Name: Tokyo Business Hub

Reason: This name represents Tokyo's business community and its networking opportunities.

2. Purpose of the Site

  • To provide local business owners with resources and networking opportunities.
  • To inform the community about upcoming business-related events.
  • To encourage new businesses to join the chamber.

3. Target Audience

  • Entrepreneurs looking for business support.
  • Small business owners who want to expand their network.
  • Residents interested in local business events.

4. Color Scheme

Primary Color: #0044cc (Blue) - Represents professionalism and trust.

Accent Color: #ffcc00 (Yellow) - Represents energy and enthusiasm.

5. Typography

Fonts: Poetsen One (for all text) to give the site a bold and unique look.

6. Wireframe

The following wireframes illustrate the layout of the website:

Mobile View

For mobile devices, the layout follows a vertical structure with a hamburger menu for navigation.

Desktop View

For larger screens, the navigation menu is fully visible at the top, and content is arranged in multiple columns to utilize more space.
